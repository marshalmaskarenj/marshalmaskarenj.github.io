Hi. I'm Marshal Maskarenj, Ph.D.

I am a building energy scientist and educator, interested in various aspects of building lighting. I have recently found love for Grasshopper, Python, and Non-Image-Forming effects of light. I try to club all three in my work.
I am a FNRS postdoc working at LOCI/LAB, UCLouvain. Previously I have worked at IIHS Bangalore and CEPT University Ahmedabad.
Beyond research, capturing and processing light has fascinated me for quite a long time, through my experiments as a photographer.
