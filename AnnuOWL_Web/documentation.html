AnnuOWL: Annual Evaluation of Occupant Wellbeing through Lighting

AnnuOWL is developed by Marshal Maskarenj and Sergio Altomonte at LAB/LOCI UCLouvain; as part of the FNRS funded project SCALE (Shading Control Algorithms from Luminance-based Evaluations).

AnnuOWL is an opensource workflow in Rhino Grasshopper developed for performing annual evaluation of lighting performance for a built environment. The evaluated performance integrating established metrics of occupant comfort, satisfaction, health and wellbeing, along with the lighting energy use, are displayed using the intuitive OVNI diagrams.
